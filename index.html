Verify Your Certificate

OSHAS regularly receives requests from employers, recruitment agencies, Higher Education institutions, and other organizations to confirm that an individual holds a OSHAS qualification. It is important to OSHAS that organizations can validate qualifications so that you can maintain workplace safety. We take all reports of fraudulent Certificates and Parchments very seriously, and we advise all students to keep these documents secure to prevent fraud.

Qr Code

All Certificates and Parchments issued by OSHAS following examinations will include a QR code in the bottom. This code can be scanned with a smart phone to verify its authenticity.

Online Verification

Using our free self-service verification platform, you can validate all Certificates and Parchments issued by OSHAS.
